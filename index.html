Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes — Willowbrook is currently welcoming new patients of all ages, including infants and seniors. Most new-patient visits can be scheduled within the same week.

What insurance do you accept?

We're in-network with most major insurance plans. If you're unsure about your coverage, our front desk will verify your benefits before your visit at no charge.

Can I get a same-week appointment?

In most cases, yes. We hold same-week slots every day for new concerns, and urgent same-day visits are available by calling the clinic directly.

Do you offer telehealth visits?

Yes, for follow-ups, prescription renewals, and many general consultations. Your care team will let you know if an in-person visit is recommended instead.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Bring a photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, and any recent records from a previous provider if you have them.
